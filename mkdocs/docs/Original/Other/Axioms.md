Epistemic First Principles:

Rules of inference

- Subjective
    - Perceptions -> Reality
    - Memories -> Past Perceptions
- Deductive
- Abductive
	- Falsifiability
	- Generality
	- Parisomony (Occam's Razor)
	- Explanatory
- Inductive
    - Past -> Future
    - Observed -> Unobserved
- Constructive
    - Modal
    - Counterfactual
    - Probabilistic
    - Causation
- Normative
    - Goals -> Reasons
    - Future Reasons -> Current Reasons
    - Others' Reasons -> Agent's Reasons
